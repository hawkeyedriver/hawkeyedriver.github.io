Embedded Programming

Welcome to the next step in following the pathway of computer science. This is the course that determines your continuation in one or more of these pathways. This class involes a lot of self-directed study, with several students across all classes asked to step in as leaders by serving as Teachers Assistants as needed. You will be performing a series of embedded programming task, including standing up a first-in-county internal intranet data center!
